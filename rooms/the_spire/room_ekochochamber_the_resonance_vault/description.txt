Within seconds of stepping through, a researcher feels the weight of the ship’s hull fade, replaced by an intimate, underwater‑like ambience that seems to swallow sound and amplify thought.

The process is part archive, part oracle: a living sound library where dead worlds speak their final words before being reborn. The teal illumination is soft but steady, making the entire room feel as though it is suspended within a vast, silent sea.
