Catalyst Synthesis Chamber

The Forge of Planetary Transformation

Description

The Catalyst Synthesis Chamber sprawls beneath the vaulted arches of Ombra Prime's Industrial Domain, a cavernous hall that feels more like a subterranean forge than a sterile laboratory. Its name is etched into the massive blast doors in stark, utilitarian lettering, and a narrow, spiraling maintenance staircase descends from the upper deck directly into the chamber's core. The room is a vertical canyon of steel and stone, its ceiling lost in a lattice of pipes and rail-mounted robotic arms that criss-cross like the skeletal ribs of a giant beast. At its heart the chamber serves the ship's life-giving purpose: it refines raw planetary catalysts—meteoritic ore, volatile gases, and trace mineral deposits harvested during planetary surveys—into the precise atmospheric compounds that will later be dispersed by The Breather across dead worlds. Three colossal fusion vats dominate the centre, each a towering cylinder of heat-resistant alloy, their interiors glowing a fierce, molten orange as they superheat the raw material. The lighting is deliberately kept in near-total darkness, save for the aggressive amber glow of the fusion vats, which paints the chamber in a perpetual twilight of orange and black. That glow is intermittently broken by the flash of arcing electricity when a mix reaches a critical point, and by the soft, pulsing luminescence of the containment runes etched into the floor. Those runes, drawn in a silvery-blue phosphorescent paint, throb in rhythm with Oris's watchful eye, indicating the AI's constant surveillance of pressure, temperature, and chemical stability. Robotic arms suspended from ceiling rails move with hypnotic precision, their articulated claws tending to the delicate chemical processes. The air is thick with metallic vapor that clings to the skin, forcing every worker to don sealed respiratory gear before entering.
