ASSET: The Dewpoint

A personal atmospheric regulator worn as a face mask by workers in the lower zones. Nano-membrane technology creates a microclimate of humidified, ionized air around the wearer's face. The device hums softly, releasing a fine mist that counteracts the bone-dry recycled air of the mechanical levels. Essential equipment for anyone working below The City.

Logbook Entry - Day 2,187 of the Voyage

The Gut hums tonight. I can feel it in my teeth. Third shift in the Root Weave, checking couplings at Junction 7. I stopped at the last alcove to top off my Dewpoint.

The air tastes like nothing. Recycled nothing. My throat closed up the first week until I got my regulator calibrated. Now I barely notice the soft hiss against my chin. Just the cool mist when I breathe deep.

The Dewpoint glows a faint blue in the darkness. An older model, the Settlement-era kind with brass clasps. Chen says they're phasing them out for polymer masks, but I don't trust anything that doesn't have a little weight to it. Something real to hold onto when the void presses in from below.

The corridor ahead is black as pitch except for my helmet lamp and distant red emergency markers. Somewhere far below, the Cathedral's thermal exchangers cycle. Oris is awake down there. Thinking her deep thoughts in the dark.

I adjust my Dewpoint. The mist is perfect tonight—cool, carrying a hint of trace electrolyte. Someone in the Pearl loaded it last month. Said it helps with the bone-deep chill you get down here.

They're not wrong. But you don't come to the Root Weave for comfort. You come because something needs fixing.

The mission continues.

Sera Vance
Maintenance Tech, Third Shift
Root Weave Sector 7
