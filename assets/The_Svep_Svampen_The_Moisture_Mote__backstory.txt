ASSET: The Svep Svampen (The Moisture Mote)

A handheld bio-sensor for farmers in The Pearl. Mycelium-composite shell shifts color with humidity; bioluminescent spore clusters glow brighter from amber to cyan as hydration rises.

---

LOGBOOK ENTRY - Keiko Tann, Junior Mycologist

Location: The Pearl, Section 7 - Fungal Terrace 3

Date: Day 2,147 of the Crossing

---

The pod is quiet this morning. Condensation drips from the dome ceiling. Svep Svampen pulses in my palm as I press it to the soil near the Aurelia caps. The glow shifts pale yellow to warm green. Good. The roots are drinking well.

The artificial sun begins its climb. Amber light filters through mist. The distant hum of water recyclers sounds like breathing. Sometimes I forget we're moving through nothing at all.

Mira taught me to read the colors. "The svampen never lies. It is grown from the same mycelium as the crops. It understands." I thought she was being poetic, but after six months I think she might be right. She is always right.

Somewhere below, Oris runs the factories in the dark. Somewhere above, children play under amber street lights. But here, in Section 7, it is just the pod, the light, and the quiet work of things that grow.

That is enough. That has to be enough.

End of entry.
