**Asset Name: Vitalis Resonator Ring**

A small, worn ring made from recycled bio-ceramic from The Pearl. Workers in the lower zones wear these to maintain a subtle connection to the Loom's energy flow. The ring contains a micro-amount of dormant Flora-frequencies absorbed during its owner's time in the biodomes. When activated by body heat, it emits a faint green pulse visible through the skin, a reminder that life exists beyond the pipes and metal.

---

**Logbook Entry:**

The morning shift starts at 0600. I wake to the sound of my own breathing, thick and stale in the recycled air of Sector 7.

My Resonator Ring is cold on my finger. I hold it to my lips, exhale, and watch the green light flicker once. Still alive. Still connected.

Down here, the machines never sleep. The hum of the Industrial Domain presses against my skull like a second heartbeat. Oris watches everything from the shadows, her presence felt in the precise rhythm of every automated arm, every conveyor belt. I am a guest in her domain.

But my ring pulses. Green. Terra. Reminds me of the morning I spent in The Pearl, walking beneath the inverted trees, breathing air that tasted like rain. My daughter held my hand there. She was seven years old.

Now she is twelve. She barely remembers the surface anymore.

The factory siren sounds. Time to feed the machines. My ring pulses again, and I follow it into the dark.

Tonight, I will bring it back to the surface. I will hold it to the sunlight and let it remember what green means.
